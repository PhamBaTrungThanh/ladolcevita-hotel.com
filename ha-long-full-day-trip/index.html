Ha Long Full Day Trip

by ladolcevita%A %B %e%q, %Y

Halong Bay is one of Vietnam’s most precious treasures. The Bay was awarded one of the new 7 wonders of the World in 2011. Each year, thousands and millions of visitors make their trip to Vietnam in order to enjoy the pure and beautiful creation created by million years of the earth evolution. Mother Nature has biased Vietnam by giving us one of Her best pieces of art. And we want you, our friends, to have a chance to get to know the real Halong bay, the destination that gets the world talk about.

We would like to suggest that you make the most of your travel experience by taking advantages on our offers for the popular and high standard Halong Bay Cruises. Let us help you to achieve the best value for the money.

 Details:

+ Daily Departure

+ Leisurely cruise
Morning:
8.00am: 4 hours – Road trip to Ha Long Bay City through Red River delta. Enjoy the scenery of tranquil villages embellished by peaceful green paddy field in the rural Vietnam. 12.00 noon arrive in Ha Long City, get on the Boat and have lunch on boat.

Afternoon:
13.00am:  Cruise about 4 hours through a maze of islets amid dramatic natural scenery. Passing through the Rocky Dog, Dinh Huong islet, Duck islet, Cock fighting islet while having lunch. Visit Thien Cung & Dau Go Grottoes. Enjoy the cruise around the bay on the return before taking car back to Hanoi.
19h.00 Arrive in Hanoi. End services.

Including features:

Transport Shuttle Bus
Guide Experienced English Speaking guide
Activities Kayak / Bamboo boat
Food Vietnamese Meals
Other Entrance tickets
